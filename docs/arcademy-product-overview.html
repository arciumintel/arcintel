Introduction

Executive Summary

Arcademy is onboarding infrastructure built for ecosystem adoption. It closes the gap between static documentation and real user readiness by guiding people through structured programs and confirming understanding.

Arcademy turns onboarding into a measurable system: guided programs, partner-ready program creation tools, and immutable proof of understanding through a soulbound award.

Core Feature Modules

Each module focuses on clear outcomes and practical value for users and partners.

Guided Onboarding Programs

Programs move people through content in a clear order so they always know what to do next.

  • Reduces confusion and drop-off.
  • Gives users a predictable learning path.
  • Improves time-to-confidence for new users.

Program Creation Tooling

Partners and teams can create, organize, and improve onboarding programs without technical complexity.

  • Faster launch of partner programs.
  • Consistent quality across all materials.
  • Simple updates with governed publishing flow.

Proof of Understanding

Completion means users proved they understood the material. Qualified users receive an immutable soulbound token tied to verified completion.

  • Builds trust in completion results.
  • Helps partners identify ready users.
  • Creates durable proof that cannot be transferred.

Partner and User Visibility

Arcademy gives both users and partners clear visibility into progress, readiness, and where support is needed.

  • Shows progress in a simple, actionable way.
  • Highlights where users get stuck.
  • Supports better partner planning and support decisions.
